Accessibility in Web Development:

Accessibility in web development ensures websites are designed and coded to be usable by all individuals, including those with disabilities, providing equal access to information and functionality.

Importance and Benefits:

  1. Inclusivity: Accessible websites include everyone, fostering a more inclusive online environment.
  2. Legal Compliance: Complying with accessibility standards ensures adherence to legal requirements in many countries.
  3. Enhanced User Experience: Accessible design leads to improved usability for all users, not just those with disabilities.
  4. Expanded Audience: Accessible websites accommodate users with various disabilities, expanding the potential audience.
  5. Positive Reputation: Demonstrating commitment to inclusivity enhances a brand's reputation and social responsibility.
